About The Event

The demand of fuzzy clustering is increasing as the use of artificial intelligence is growing in the world. To share the knowledge of such significant topic of Artificial Intelligence, the AI division of VIT Bhopal is organizing a webinar on “Collaborative Fuzzy Clustering and its Application” by Dr. Mukesh Prasad who is faculty of Engineering and IT in the University of Technology in Sydney, Australia on 21 December 2021 from 3 - 4:30 pm.
