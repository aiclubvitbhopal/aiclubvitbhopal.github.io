The Last Hunt will be a two-day event from 2 October 2021- 3 October 2021. This event will be one of its kind in VIT Bhopal; with an aim to incorporate a broad understanding of AI among the students along with intriguing and entertaining aspects through its Marvel-based theme.

OUR SCHEDULE

Day-1

Round-1

All teams will participate in a brief quiz, which will serve as a qualifying round. The best 15 teams will advance to Round 2

Round-2 : Beat the timer round (2 sub-rounds)

Each team must answer a question; if that team gives an incorrect answer, that team will be rewarded with a negative point; if that team fails to answer the question in the allotted time, the question will be passed to the next team as a bonus question.

Day-2

Round 3: Debugging round:

Teams will be given 8-10 programs with problems, which they must debug and compile in a restricted time limit in order to receive the final round clues and links. All of the links they will receive after debugging will not be useful or right, thus the teams that receive the proper links will be chosen for the final round.

Round 4

Teams must open the link and locate the repositories for the question. Once they have located the correct repository, they will be given two competitive coding questions and a ppt topic. The team that provides the most accurate answer and has the most thoroughly researched ppt will be declared as the winner.
